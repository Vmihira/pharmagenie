PharmaGenie Terms and Conditions

1. Pricing and Cost

All medicines purchased through PharmaGenie must be bought at a minimum of 30% of their original price.

2. Medicine Expiry Check

It is the responsibility of the pharmacy store to verify the expiry date of medicines before purchasing them from people. Expired medicines must not be accepted under any circumstances.

3. Delivery Options and Cost Variation

Cost Based on Delivery Option:

  • With Delivery: If a delivery option is provided, the medicine must be purchased at 30% of the original price.
  • Without Delivery: If no delivery option is provided, the pharmacy must purchase the medicine at 35% of the original price.

4. Delivery Option Finality

Once an order has been accepted, the chosen delivery option cannot be altered under any circumstances.

5. Delivery Tracking

Pharmacy stores must continuously track the delivery person until the medicines have been properly purchased from the seller and brought back to the pharmacy.

6. Liability Disclaimer

PharmaGenie is not responsible for the efficacy or failure of any medicines purchased through the platform. It is the sole responsibility of the pharmacy store to check the expiry and quality of medicines before completing any purchase.
